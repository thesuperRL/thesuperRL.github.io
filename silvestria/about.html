About, Contact, and Frequently Asked Questions

Who are we?

Silvestria (Latin for "Woodland") is a small local, online store started by two high school friends in the end of their sophomore year.
This is their first business together, and with Silvestria they hope to create a change in this stagnating world, one product at a time.

What is Silvestria?

Silvestria is a company that Ryan and Nathan co-founded. Throughout Ryan's 9th grade year, he made many items, 3D printed, laser cut, or vinyl cut, giving them all to his friends and family. Many of his friends jokingly said "you should sell this!"

Little did they know, these lighthearted comments would give birth to a new idea - a company where Ryan can use his own skills to give back to his community. Ryan came up with the idea of the company during the initial months of his sophomore year, though he was too busy to implement it during most of his sophomore year. Towards the last few months, on a car ride to Nathan's house for work on finals, they created a wonderful vision of a new company - one that sells products Ryan makes in order to return the profits to the community. They initially called it "Woodland Warmth".


What is EPS?

EPS, Eastside Preparatory School, is the school that both Ryan and Nathan go to. All of the Silvestria products are made with the resources and machines that EPS provides. Even the CAD software that Ryan uses, Autodesk Fusion 360, is thanks to the student access that EPS procured. That's why Silvestria donates half of its "profits" to the school, as thanks for materials and machines, as well as giving back to the community which helped "create" Ryan and Nathan. This is the place where Ryan and Nathan first met, and where they created this company after almost two full years of friendship.


Why can we only preorder?

Because since Silvestria uses EPS materials and machines, we cannot make products outside of school. That's why we keep a list of orders so that we can make them when school next starts, around the beginning of September.


Who can order?

So far, only members of the EPS community and their relatives, since Silvestria currently doesn't have the means to deliver yet (we're working on that, we promise).
