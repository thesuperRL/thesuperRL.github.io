About, Contact, and Frequently Asked Questions

Who are we?

Silvestria (Latin for "Woodland") is a small local, online store started by two high school friends in the end of their sophomore year.
This is their first business together, and with Silvestria they hope to create a change in this stagnating world, one product at a time.

Changrui (Ryan) Li

Hi, I'm Ryan! I'm interested in creating crafts around the EPS makerspace, and I love using my 3D modeling talent to solve problems and create neat decorations. In Silvestria, I create the products in CAD and bring them into reality for customers. I also made this website! I wanted to start this company with Nathan because it allows us to give back to the community, and provides experience for future businesses.
