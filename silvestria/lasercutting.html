Laser Cutting:

Unrivaled Speed

Far faster than 3d printing, yet able to be assembled into similar parts. If you want to make something flat or simple boxes, laser cutting is preferable over 3D printing.

Made Flat, Stored Flat

Since laser cut wood is flat, most objects made from it can be disassembled and easily stored flat when traveling. When it is damaged, you only need to purchase the damaged part. This makes storage, replacement, and disposal convienient.

Sights and Smells

Aside from simply looking natural, it brings a fireplace-like smell to wherever you place it, making it optimal for establishing a cozy, natural atmosphere in the workplace or at home.
