I like the makerspace because it has a variety of machines and materials
that we can work with. There are many mediums to express my creativity
and my interest in design. We can design the materials and create them,
or simply get models from the internet and transfer them into the real
world. I like it because it allows me to show my talents in engineering,
and create items to help myself, my peers, or even the school. As a result
of this, I spend most of my time within the makerspace.
